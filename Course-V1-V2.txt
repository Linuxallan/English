APUNTES

Noun : palabra que sirve para nombrar un material o un inmaterial.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
VIDEOS

1. noun + is + noun ->> An ant is an insect
	a book
	an animal
- Exceptions
	a university
	an hour

2. Plural noun + are + plural noun ->> Ants are insects
	a city ->> cities
	a country ->> countries
- Exceptions
	tray ->> trays
